Priscilla Nunes

3D Artist | Archviz Artista 3D

My name is Priscilla Nunes, I'm Brazilian, graduated in Architecture and Urbanism in Brazil and also in Interior Design, through a professional course in Portugal. I've already done a 1-year exchange program in the USA, where I had the opportunity to study Architecture at the University of Arkansas, and also to do a Civil Engineering training course at California State University, Long Beach. I currently work remotely as a 3D Artist. That is, I do 3d modeling and rendering for professionals in the field construction companies and real estate agents from anywhere in the world. In addition to this professional part, my favorite hobbies are traveling, watching series and movies, listening to music and meditating.
